St Albans' Period Window Experts

St Albans is one of England's most historic cities, with architecture spanning from medieval times to the present day. The city centre features stunning Georgian townhouses, elegant Victorian properties, and charming Edwardian homes, all requiring windows that respect their architectural heritage. At Skylon Timber & Glazing, we craft authentic timber sash windows that preserve St Albans' unique character.

St Albans has extensive Conservation Areas protecting its historic core, including the Cathedral area, Fishpool Street, and the Victorian residential areas. Many properties also hold Listed Building status. Our experienced team understands the requirements of St Albans City and District Council and creates windows that satisfy heritage officers while delivering modern performance.

Why St Albans Homeowners Choose Us

Our timber sash windows are crafted exclusively from premium hardwood and engineered timber, preventing warping in the variable Hertfordshire climate. We use traditional joinery techniques combined with modern performance features including slimline double glazing, draught-proofing, and PAS24 security certification.

Every window is finished with our premium spray system using Sikkens Wood Coatings and Teknos products, ensuring a flawless finish that lasts for decades. As FENSA registered installers, we handle everything from initial survey to final installation, giving you complete peace of mind.
