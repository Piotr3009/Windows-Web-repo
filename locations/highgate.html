Highgate's Period Window Experts

Highgate Village retains a unique charm with its historic high street, elegant Victorian villas, and picturesque Georgian cottages. Perched on one of London's highest points with views across the city, Highgate's properties are treasured for their architectural character. At Skylon Timber & Glazing, we craft authentic timber sash windows that preserve and enhance Highgate's distinctive village atmosphere.

Highgate contains several protected Conservation Areas spanning both Haringey and Camden boroughs. Our team has experience working across both council jurisdictions, understanding the specific requirements for properties around Highgate Village, Highgate Hill, and the areas surrounding Waterlow Park and Highgate Cemetery.

Why Highgate Homeowners Choose Us

Our timber sash windows are crafted exclusively from premium hardwood and engineered timber, preventing warping in London's variable climate. We use traditional joinery techniques combined with modern performance features including slimline double glazing, draught-proofing, and PAS24 security certification.

Every window is finished with our premium spray system using Sikkens Wood Coatings and Teknos products, ensuring a flawless finish that lasts for decades. As FENSA registered installers, we handle everything from initial survey to final installation, giving you complete peace of mind.
