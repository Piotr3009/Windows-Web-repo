Crouch End's Period Window Experts

Crouch End is beloved for its stunning Edwardian architecture, with tree-lined streets of substantial family homes featuring distinctive period details. The area's Victorian terraces and impressive Edwardian villas create one of North London's most desirable neighbourhoods. At Skylon Timber & Glazing, we specialise in crafting authentic timber sash windows that complement Crouch End's architectural character.

Crouch End sits within Haringey's Conservation Area framework, with particular attention paid to preserving the Edwardian character of streets around the Broadway, Hornsey Lane, and Coolhurst Road. Our team understands the local requirements and creates windows that honour the area's heritage while providing modern performance benefits.

Why Crouch End Homeowners Choose Us

Our timber sash windows are crafted exclusively from premium hardwood and engineered timber, preventing warping in London's variable climate. We use traditional joinery techniques combined with modern performance features including slimline double glazing, draught-proofing, and PAS24 security certification.

Every window is finished with our premium spray system using Sikkens Wood Coatings and Teknos products, ensuring a flawless finish that lasts for decades. As FENSA registered installers, we handle everything from initial survey to final installation, giving you complete peace of mind.
