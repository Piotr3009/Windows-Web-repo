Potters Bar's Period Window Experts

Potters Bar offers an attractive mix of Edwardian, inter-war, and Victorian properties, set within easy reach of London whilst enjoying the benefits of the Hertfordshire countryside. The town's period homes, particularly around the older areas near the station and along The Walk, require windows that complement their traditional character. At Skylon Timber & Glazing, we craft authentic timber sash windows that enhance Potters Bar's architectural charm.

Potters Bar falls under Hertsmere Borough Council, which takes heritage matters seriously. Properties in the older parts of town and near the Green Belt boundary often require careful consideration of planning requirements. Our team understands these local considerations and creates windows that meet both heritage expectations and building regulations.

Why Potters Bar Homeowners Choose Us

Our timber sash windows are crafted exclusively from premium hardwood and engineered timber, preventing warping in the variable Hertfordshire climate. We use traditional joinery techniques combined with modern performance features including slimline double glazing, draught-proofing, and PAS24 security certification.

Every window is finished with our premium spray system using Sikkens Wood Coatings and Teknos products, ensuring a flawless finish that lasts for decades. As FENSA registered installers, we handle everything from initial survey to final installation, giving you complete peace of mind.
