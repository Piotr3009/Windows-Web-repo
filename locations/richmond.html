Richmond's Period Window Experts

Richmond upon Thames is one of London's most prestigious boroughs, famous for its stunning Georgian architecture, riverside properties, and the magnificent Richmond Park. The elegant townhouses along Richmond Green, the grand villas of Richmond Hill, and the period properties throughout the area represent some of London's finest architecture. At Skylon Timber & Glazing, we craft exceptional timber sash windows worthy of Richmond's distinguished homes.

Richmond contains numerous Conservation Areas and many Listed Buildings, with particularly strict controls around Richmond Green, Richmond Hill, and the riverside areas. Our craftsmen have extensive experience working within these protected zones, creating windows that satisfy both the Richmond Council heritage officers and the high expectations of Richmond homeowners.

Why Richmond Homeowners Choose Us

Our timber sash windows are crafted exclusively from premium hardwood and engineered timber, preventing warping in London's variable climate. We use traditional joinery techniques combined with modern performance features including slimline double glazing, draught-proofing, and PAS24 security certification.

Every window is finished with our premium spray system using Sikkens Wood Coatings and Teknos products, ensuring a flawless finish that lasts for decades. As FENSA registered installers, we handle everything from initial survey to final installation, giving you complete peace of mind.
