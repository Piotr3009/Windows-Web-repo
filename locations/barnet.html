Barnet's Period Window Experts

Barnet, with its historic High Street and surrounding Victorian and Edwardian neighbourhoods, represents the best of North London suburban living. From the period properties of High Barnet to the leafy streets of Totteridge, these homes deserve windows that reflect their architectural heritage. At Skylon Timber & Glazing, we craft authentic timber sash windows that enhance Barnet's distinctive character.

The Barnet borough includes several Conservation Areas protecting its historic character, including Monken Hadley, High Barnet, and Totteridge. Our team has extensive experience working with Barnet Council's planning requirements, ensuring windows are designed to meet heritage standards while providing modern thermal and security performance.

Why Barnet Homeowners Choose Us

Our timber sash windows are crafted exclusively from premium hardwood and engineered timber, preventing warping in London's variable climate. We use traditional joinery techniques combined with modern performance features including slimline double glazing, draught-proofing, and PAS24 security certification.

Every window is finished with our premium spray system using Sikkens Wood Coatings and Teknos products, ensuring a flawless finish that lasts for decades. As FENSA registered installers, we handle everything from initial survey to final installation, giving you complete peace of mind.
