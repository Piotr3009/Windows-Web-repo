Measurement Guide

How to measure your sash windows correctly

How to Measure Windows – Structural Opening

  • Measure the width at the top, middle, and bottom. Record the smallest measurement.
  • Measure the height at the left, center, and right. Record the smallest measurement.
  • Measure the depth (reveal) from outside brick to inside surface, noting obstructions.
  • Check squareness by measuring diagonally from corner to corner.
  • Measure the structural opening (brick-to-brick) for both width and height.
  • Measure the frame dimension (inside frame width).

Tools Needed

  • Tape measure
  • Spirit level
  • Pencil
  • Notepad
